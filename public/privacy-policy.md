# Privacy Policy for NoMoreFOMO

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

NoMoreFOMO ("we," "our," or "us") operates an AI chat platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (via Google OAuth or email signup)
- Name and profile picture (from Google OAuth, if provided)
- Authentication tokens

### Usage Data
- Chat messages and conversations
- Model selections and preferences
- Token usage and request counts
- Timestamps and session data
- IP addresses and device information

### Analytics Data
We collect anonymized, aggregated data about:
- Feature usage patterns
- Model performance metrics
- Error rates and system health
- User engagement metrics

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Service Delivery**: To provide and maintain our AI chat service
2. **Account Management**: To manage your account and subscription
3. **Improvements**: To improve our service and user experience
4. **Support**: To respond to your requests and provide customer support

### Data Monetization
**Important: By using NoMoreFOMO, you consent to the following:**

We may use **anonymized, aggregated data** derived from your usage for:
- **Analytics products**: Selling insights about AI model performance, usage patterns, and industry trends
- **Research**: Contributing to AI research and benchmarking studies
- **Business intelligence**: Licensing aggregated data to third parties

**What This Means:**
- ✅ **Anonymized**: Your data is stripped of all personally identifiable information
- ✅ **Aggregated**: Combined with data from many users, making individual identification impossible
- ❌ **Never Sold**: Your personal information, individual messages, or identifiable data is NEVER sold or shared

**Example of Anonymized Data:**
- "60% of users prefer Model X for coding tasks"
- "Average response time for casual queries: 2.3 seconds"
- "Token usage increased 15% month-over-month"

**What We DON'T Share:**
- Your email address or identity
- Your individual conversations or messages
- Any personally identifiable information

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Anonymized Data**: May be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws:

### Right to Access
Request a copy of your personal data

### Right to Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data (anonymized aggregated data excluded)

### Right to Object
Object to processing of your data for analytics purposes

### Right to Opt-Out
You can opt-out of analytics data collection (this may limit service features)
